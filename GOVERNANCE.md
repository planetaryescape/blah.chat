# Governance

This document describes the governance model for blah.chat.

## Roles

### Creator

**Bhekani Khumalo** ([@bhekanik](https://github.com/bhekanik))

- Final decision authority on project direction
- Approves new maintainers
- Resolves disputes

### Lead Maintainer

**Julien Paroche** ([@darul75](https://github.com/darul75))

- Day-to-day project management
- PR review and merge authority
- Issue triage and roadmap planning

### Contributors

Anyone who has contributed code, documentation, or other improvements via merged PRs.

## Decision Making

This project follows a **BDFL** (Benevolent Dictator For Life) model:

1. **Routine decisions** (bug fixes, small features): Lead maintainer approves
2. **Significant changes** (architecture, breaking changes): Creator approval required
3. **Disputes**: Escalate to creator for final decision

## Becoming a Maintainer

1. Demonstrate sustained, quality contributions
2. Show understanding of project goals and codebase
3. Receive invitation from creator

## Code of Conduct

All participants must follow our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

## Changes to Governance
